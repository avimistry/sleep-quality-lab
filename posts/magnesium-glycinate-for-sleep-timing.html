Frequently asked questions

Can I take magnesium glycinate with melatonin?

Yes — there is no known negative interaction. Many people use both. If combining, take magnesium 30–60 min before bed; melatonin 30 min before.

Does magnesium glycinate cause vivid dreams?

Some users report more vivid or memorable dreams, likely due to improved REM sleep quality rather than any direct pharmacological effect on dreaming.
